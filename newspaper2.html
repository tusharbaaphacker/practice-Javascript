"Bhai-Lang", New Programming Language By 2 Indians, Amuses Internet TS News

Published on: September 5, 2024

A new programming language is taking the internet by storm. “Bhai-lang” is a toy programming language created by two Indian developers - Aniket Singh from Amazon and Rishabh Tripathi from Groww. “Bhai” is Indian word for brother. It is written in Typescript and combines “bhai” from Hindi language with hi, bye and other words in English.
